# Kazkar.ai — Competitive Intelligence

## Direct competitors

### Craig Bot
- **What**: Discord recording bot
- **Strengths**: Well-known, free, reliable recording
- **Weaknesses**: Recording only — no transcription, no summarization, no lore
- **Our angle**: "Craig records. Kazkar remembers."

### Otter.ai
- **What**: General-purpose meeting transcription
- **Strengths**: Good transcription quality, well-funded
- **Weaknesses**: Not TTRPG-aware, no lore extraction, no Discord integration, expensive
- **Our angle**: Built for sessions, not meetings

### Manual note-taking (Google Docs, Notion, Obsidian)
- **What**: Players/DMs writing notes by hand
- **Strengths**: Free, flexible, personal
- **Weaknesses**: Requires attention during session, incomplete, DM can't run game and take notes
- **Our angle**: Automatic — play your game, Kazkar handles the rest

### World Anvil / LegendKeeper / Kanka
- **What**: Worldbuilding/campaign management platforms
- **Strengths**: Rich wiki features, community, templates
- **Weaknesses**: Manual entry required, no session recording, no automatic population
- **Our angle**: Wiki builds itself from your actual sessions

## Indirect competitors

### YouTube/Twitch VODs
- Some groups just record and post the full session
- No searchability, no lore extraction, hours of raw audio

### ChatGPT/Claude for session recaps
- DMs paste notes into AI for cleanup
- Requires manual input, no recording, no speaker identification
